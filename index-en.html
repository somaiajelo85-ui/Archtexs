About Us

From the heart of Damascus, we understand that a home is not just walls, but a space that pulsates with warmth and memories. At ARCHTEXS, we work to transform our clients' architectural vision into a perfect reality that combines engineering quality and aesthetic touch, to deliver spaces that exude comfort, luxury, and perfection.

We adhere to the highest standards of interior finishing and possess the expertise to execute residential projects at a professional level that meets the client's ambitions.

Our Vision

To be the leading company in the field of finishing and decor in the region, by providing integrated solutions that combine engineering creativity, precision in execution, and quality that lasts for many years.

We strive to create spaces that express our clients' personalities and reflect the aesthetics of modern architecture.
